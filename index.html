Biography

Currently I am a postdoctoral research fellow at Institute of Microelectronics, Tsinghua University, China, working with Prof. Leibo Liu and Prof. Shaojun Wei.

I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Department of Computer Science and Engineering in the Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2017. My supervisors are Prof. Ho-fung Leung and Prof. Terrence Mak. Before that, I obtained my Bachelor degree from Honors School at Harbin Institute of Technology in 2011, and Master degree from Astronautics School at Harbin Institute of Technology in 2013. I was a visiting researcher in the School of Electronics and Computer Science, University of Southampton during Mar. 2016 to Sep. 2016.

My research interests include network-on-chip, reconfigurable system, many-core system, reliability-aware design, etc.
